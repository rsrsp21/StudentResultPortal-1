How to Calculate CGPA

CGPA Formula

CGPA is calculated as the weighted average of all semester SGPAs, with credits as weights.

Converting to Percentage

Percentage = (CGPA - 0.75) × 10

Class Division

≥ 7.75: Distinction | ≥ 6.75: First Class | ≥ 5.75: Second Class | ≥ 5.0: Pass
